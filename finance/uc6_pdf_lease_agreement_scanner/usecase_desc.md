o set up the new leases in our lease accounting system, we scan through the lease agreement to figure out the following details:

1. Commencement date
2. Term length and end date
3. Leased property address or details of the leased item (e.g. VIN for vehicles, equipment) 
4. Base rental amount for each period (including fixed escalations, e.g. annual increases) and payment frequency
5. Whether there are any variable increases to the base rental amount such as CPI based increase
6. Any other fixed payments
7. Renewal options: term and number of options. Are renewals automatic?
8. Is there an option to purchase / buyout at the end of term? If yes, amount payable.
9. Is the agreement signed by both the lessor and the lessee?
